Know Your Contract allows developers to acquire user trust in their projects by allowing them to KYContract their teams and verified contract. Using Decentralized Identifiers, AI and zkProofs, KYContract attributes and mints a trust score so users know they are interacting with trusted teams. Developers are then able to display the KYContract badge of trust on their dApps.
